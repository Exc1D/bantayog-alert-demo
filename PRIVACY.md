# Privacy Policy & GDPR Compliance

## Data Collection Policy

### What We Collect

Bantayog Alert collects and processes the following personal data:

- **Account Information**: Email address, display name, municipality
- **Profile Data**: Profile picture, role, account creation date
- **Report Data**: Hazard reports you submit, including location data, photos, videos
- **Usage Data**: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service
- **Authentication Data**: Firebase Authentication tokens for account management

### How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

1. **Service Delivery**: To provide hazard alert and reporting services
2. **Account Management**: To create and manage your user account
3. **Report Processing**: To process, verify, and respond to submitted reports
4. **Communication**: To send important notifications about your account and reports
5. **Service Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
6. **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

### Legal Basis

We process your personal data based on:

- **Consent**: When you create an account or submit reports
- **Contract**: To provide our services as outlined in our Terms of Service
- **Legitimate Interest**: For service improvement and security purposes
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with applicable laws

## Your Rights Under GDPR

As a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or under applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

### Right to Access (Article 15)

You have the right to obtain confirmation as to whether or not personal data concerning you is being processed, and where that is the case, access to the following information:

- The purposes of the processing
- The categories of personal data concerned
- The recipients or categories of recipient
- The envisaged period for which the personal data will be stored
- The existence of the right to request rectification or erasure

### Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You have the right to have inaccurate personal data rectified. You may also have incomplete personal data completed.

### Right to Erasure (Article 17)

Also known as the "right to be forgotten," you have the right to have your personal data erased when:

- The personal data is no longer necessary
- You withdraw consent
- You object to the processing
- The personal data has been unlawfully processed

### Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and to transmit that data to another controller.

### Right to Object (Article 21)

You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests or public task.

### Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7)

Where processing is based on consent, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.

## Data Retention Policy

### How Long We Keep Your Data

| Data Category        | Retention Period                                |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Account Data         | Until account deletion or 2 years of inactivity |
| Submitted Reports    | 7 years for historical records                  |
| Verification Records | 7 years after report resolution                 |
| Audit Logs           | 5 years for compliance purposes                 |
| Anonymous Data       | Indefinite (aggregated, non-identifiable)       |

### What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you request account deletion:

1. Your account credentials are removed from Firebase Authentication
2. Your user profile is deleted from Firestore
3. Your profile Storage picture is removed from
4. Your submitted reports are anonymized (reporter info removed)
5. An audit log entry is created for compliance purposes

## Data Protection Measures

### Security Implementation

- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest
- **Access Control**: Role-based access control (RBAC) for admin functions
- **Audit Logging**: Comprehensive audit trail of all data access and modifications
- **Data Minimization**: We collect only what is necessary for service delivery
- **Anonymization**: Reports are anonymized after account deletion
